Hello! I'm Nuha. I'm a recent high school graduate (with some time on my hands) looking to explore my intellectual interests independently while supervising my ever-elusive cat (pictured). Welcome to my website!

This website is mainly a passion project stemming from my interest in psychiatric research and its implications, specfically how data precisely quantifies the complex, multiomic field psychiatry is. Eventually, I aim to help bridge the uncertainty in biological systems through the precision of data.

Current sitemap consists of synthesis, where I plan on carrying out literature synthesis of research papers on various topics, and dissection, where I hope to break down (or "dissect") (mathematical) concepts I encountered while self-studying. This is still very much a WIP so please be patient! Often pages may be under construction and therefore inaccessible (as I fiddle around with the UI and such), but I do hope to keep the site up and running for as long and as frequently as possible.

Enjoy your stay!
